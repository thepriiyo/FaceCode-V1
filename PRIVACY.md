# FaceCode V1 | Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-16

FaceCode V1 is designed with a **Privacy-First Architecture**. We respect your privacy and provide this disclosure to explain our data practices.

## 1. Zero Data Collection
FaceCode V1 **does not collect, store, or transmit** any of your personal data. 
- **Camera Access:** Used solely to generate real-time visual effects within your browser.
- **Microphone Access:** Used solely to modulate visual parameters based on live audio frequency.
- **Images/Videos:** Any snapshots or recordings you make are processed locally and saved directly to your device. We have no access to your content.

## 2. 100% Client-Side Processing
All visual and audio processing occurs on your local device (CPU/GPU). No data is ever sent to a server, cloud service, or third-party provider. 

## 3. Permissions
FaceCode V1 requires camera and microphone permissions to function as intended. These permissions are used exclusively for real-time visual synthesis. You may revoke these permissions at any time through your browser settings.

## 4. Local Storage
FaceCode V1 uses your browser's `localStorage` to save your visual synthesizer presets (colors, shifts, resolution). This data remains on your machine and is never shared.

---
*FaceCode V1 is provided "as-is" for creative use. By using the application, you agree to this local-only privacy protocol.*
