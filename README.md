## 🎨 The Instrument: Operational Manual

### ⚡ The Visual Synthesizer HUD
The core of FaceCode is its tactile operator dashboard. Click the **⚡** icon to toggle the deep-synthesis controls:

- **CHROMATIC_SHIFT:** Precision RGB displacement. Use this to create "liquid light" effects and edge-fringing that pulses with the image.
- **GHOST_TRAILS:** Implements a temporal feedback loop. High values create a "long-exposure" smear, perfect for high-motion performances.
- **KALEIDOSCOPE:** Mirrors your feed across up to 12 axes of symmetry. Transforms a simple face-shot into complex, geometric sacred-geometry art.
- **GLITCH_ENTROPY:** Injects real-time signal noise. High values cause the ASCII characters to randomly "re-order" or jitter, creating a digital-grind aesthetic.

### 🎵 Kinetic Audio Injection
FaceCode doesn't just "show" audio; it is driven by it.
- **Violet Pulse:** The entire engine "jumps" (scales) based on lower-frequency peaks (kicks/bass).
- **Brightness Flicker:** Audio gain causes instantaneous brightness spikes, allowing you to "strobe" the engine with your voice or music.
- **Gain Control:** Use the **AUDIO_INJECTION** slider to calibrate the engine's sensitivity to your environment.

### 📸 Media Master Suite
FaceCode V1 includes a professional-grade export pipeline:
- **SNAPSHOT:** Instantly crystallizes the current frame into a high-fidelity **PNG** image.
- **RECORD:** Toggle real-time recording to capture high-resolution **MP4 (H.264)** loops of your visual performances. All processing is done in-browser with zero latency.

## 📐 Optic Resolution Control
The **OPTIC_RES** slider allows you to shift the aesthetic identity of the engine:
- **Low (20px - 60px):** "Full Code" mode. Large, blocky characters that emphasize the digital structure.
- **High (200px - 400px):** "Crystal" mode. Subtle, high-fidelity ASCII that mimics the look of a vintage CRT monitor with extreme detail.

## 🛠️ Performance & Deployment
FaceCode V1 is built on a **GPU/CPU Hybrid Architecture**. It utilizes **WebGL Shaders** for speed and **Web Workers** for concurrent processing, ensuring a locked 60 FPS experience.

### Local Ignition
1. Clone this repository.
2. Serve via local server (e.g. `python -m http.server`).
3. Open `localhost` in your browser.

### Cloud Deployment
FaceCode is optimized for zero-config hosting on **Vercel** or **GitHub Pages**. Ensure your host provides an **HTTPS** context, as browsers require security to enable hardware sensors (Camera/Mic).

## ⚖️ Privacy & Safety Protocol
- **LOCKED_LOCAL:** FaceCode V1 is a 100% client-side application. **Zero camera or microphone data is ever transmitted or stored.** 
- **Photosensitivity Advisory:** FaceCode generates high-intensity visual feedback and strobes. Use with caution if you have a history of photosensitive epilepsy.
